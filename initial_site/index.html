'Deer Sighting' Snapchat Community Filter

Upon starting my university studies at the University of Toronto Mississauga, I came across many deer sightings throughout the campus environment. Everytime I come across a deer sighting, I see many other students taking advantage of the sighting to take pictures and upload them on Snapchat. This inspired me to create a community geofilter dedicated to these peaceful and lovely animals that stride their way around campus. The goal of this geofilter is to allow students to capture the special moment of encountering a deer on campus and giving them a special filter that can go along with their posts. The geofilter was created using Adobe Illustrator CC and Adobe Photoshop CC.

The geofilter is currently available to anyone with access to Snapchat on all devices and are within the university premises and parts of the surrounding area. This project is not affiliated with the University of Toronto or the Mississauga campus in any form.

Statistics as of February 10, 2017

  • 46,500+ total uses on Snapchat (including posting on public stories and sending as a private message)
  • 1,200,000+ total views on Snapchat



'Terrain Explorer' Sample Unity Game

This project was created for the Immersive Environment Design course at University of Toronto. The purpose of this project is to explore the terrain and environment tools within Unity using the built-in environment, effects and camera packages. Terrain objects were created and modified using Blender, an open-source 3D design software. The environment also implements a first-person camera in order to explore the terrain area and to interact with the environment.

This was an individual solo project. Trees and terrain textures were created by Unity.
